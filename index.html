About Us

This local community group is for any computer programmers, software developers, software engineers, web developers, or anyone interested in coding. This group is primarily compromised of local coders around the Quad-City IA/IL area.

Brian Hankins

Previous to my software development career I worked in cyber security and also served a few years in the Army, stationed in Heidelberg, Germany. In my free time I enjoy spending time with my wife and 2 kids, playing PC games, fishing, pistol shooting, and reading. I'm a huge New England Patriots and Chicago Cubs fan. A couple of my long term goals include speaking at Visual Studio Live developer conferences and to inspire students and young adults to pursue computer science careers.
